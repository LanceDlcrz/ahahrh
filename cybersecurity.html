Stay Safe Online

Protect your data, devices, and identity from cyber threats.

Why Cybersecurity Matters

Cybersecurity protects your personal information from hackers. As technology grows, online threats are becoming more advanced, making it important to stay informed and secure.

Common Threats

  • Phishing attacks
  • Malware
  • Ransomware
  • Data breaches

Essential Tips

  • Use strong passwords
  • Enable two-factor authentication
  • Avoid suspicious links
  • Keep your software updated

Security Tools

Use antivirus software, VPNs, and password managers to stay protected online.
