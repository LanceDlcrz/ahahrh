🌐 Understanding Networking

Learn how devices connect and communicate in the digital world.

What is Networking?

Networking connects computers and devices so they can share data and resources. It powers everything from home Wi-Fi to the global internet.

Types of Networks

  • LAN (Local Area Network)
  • WAN (Wide Area Network)
  • MAN (Metropolitan Area Network)

Key Devices

  • Router
  • Switch
  • Modem
  • Access Point

Basic Concepts

  • IP Address
  • Bandwidth
  • Latency
  • Protocols (HTTP, TCP/IP)

Why Networking Matters

Networking enables internet access, communication, gaming, and cloud computing.
