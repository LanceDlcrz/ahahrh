Exploring Robotics

Learn how robots are shaping the future of technology.

What is Robotics?

Robotics is the field that focuses on creating machines that can perform tasks automatically. It combines engineering, programming, and artificial intelligence.

Types of Robots

  • Industrial robots
  • Service robots
  • Autonomous robots
  • Humanoid robots

Key Components

  • Sensors
  • Actuators
  • Controllers
  • Power source

Applications

Robots are used in manufacturing, healthcare, transportation, space exploration, and smart homes.

Why Robotics Matters

Robotics improves efficiency, safety, and innovation across many industries.
